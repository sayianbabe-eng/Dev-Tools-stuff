Story To Design Goal

It streamlines the early project phases, reduces back and forth, and gives clients the ability to structure their requests to stay true to their campaign goals and brand identity.

Adaptive form

This accepts client’s input and generates guiding questions to identify: Objectives, target audiences, Brand voice, storytelling goals and deliverables.

Main Functions

  • Generates suggestions to modify the clients’ input words into language that become more coherent with their core values.
  • Has an upload section for users with documents.
  • Generate a project brief for the project for client’s review. This is an iterative process which can be modified by supplementary inputs.
